Shipping FAQs

Can I change my delivery address after placing the order?

Address changes can be made within 2 hours of placing the order by calling our support team. After dispatch, changes are not possible.

What if I miss the delivery?

Our delivery agent will attempt delivery twice. If both attempts fail, the order is returned to our warehouse. Please contact us to reschedule.

Is shipping available to all states in India?

We deliver to all major states and union territories. Some remote areas in J&K, Northeast, and island territories may have limited serviceability.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping is not available at this time.
